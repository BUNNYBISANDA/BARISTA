CH. 01 / OUR STORY

Twenty-three years of warmth.

Sri Lanka's largest café chain. Eighty-seven homes. One island.

CH. 02 / THE OPENING

How a café became a national habit.

For over twenty-three years, Barista has been at the heart of Sri Lanka's café culture — a network of eighty-seven outlets stretching from the bustling streets of Colombo to the tranquil hills of Kandy. The promise has stayed simple: better coffee, a warmer welcome, and a place that feels familiar whether it's your first visit or your thousandth.

I. THE CRAFT

Authentic Italian coffee, brewed with discipline.

Our baristas train to master the espresso, the cappuccino, the macchiato — the shots and pours that sit at the heart of an Italian café. Every cup we serve has discipline behind it; every cup tastes like it was meant to.

II. THE WELCOME

The branded experience that sets us apart.

It isn't only the coffee — it's the warm hello at the door, the help choosing the right drink, the unhurried atmosphere that makes a café feel like a third place. Whatever brings you in, we want you to leave knowing you were welcomed.

III. THE REACH

Eighty-seven cafés, across one island.

From a quick pick-me-up on a busy morning to a slow afternoon catch-up, you'll find a Barista nearby. Every district holds a familiar door — open daily, brewing the same warmth.

CH. 03 / THE PROMISE

The promise behind every cup.

OUR VISION

To make Sri Lanka a place where no one has to settle for less than the world's best coffee experience.

OUR MISSION

As the largest café chain in Sri Lanka, we will create and deliver the best value to all our stakeholders while safeguarding the interests of the environment and the community.
