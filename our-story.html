CH. 02 / HERITAGE

The brand grew one familiar visit at a time.

Barista's story is built on a simple idea made consistent at scale: better coffee, a warmer welcome, and spaces people can return to with confidence.

Welcome to Barista, Sri Lanka's largest cafe chain, where we have been proudly serving up the finest coffee and delivering unparalleled customer service for over two decades. Since our founding 23 years ago, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to excellence, ensuring that every cup of coffee we brew is crafted with the utmost care and precision.

With an impressive network of over 87 outlets strategically located across the island, from the bustling streets of Colombo to the tranquil hills of Kandy, we have become a beloved fixture in communities nationwide. Wherever you find yourself in Sri Lanka, you can always count on Barista to provide a welcoming haven where you can savor a perfectly brewed cup of coffee.

At Barista, we take pride in bringing authentic Italian coffee to Sri Lanka. Our skilled baristas undergo rigorous training to master the art of crafting the perfect cup of coffee, ensuring that every sip is a delightful experience. From classic espresso shots to velvety cappuccinos and indulgent macchiatos, our extensive menu offers a wide array of flavors to satisfy every coffee lover's cravings.

Barista is not just about the coffee; it is the entire branded customer experience that sets us apart. From the moment you walk through our doors, our friendly crew greets you with warm smiles, helps you choose the perfect beverage, and creates a welcoming atmosphere where you can relax, socialize, or find solace in your own thoughts.

CH. 03 / VISION & MISSION

The ambition, and the responsibility behind it.

The scale of the brand is matched by two simple commitments: raise the standard of the coffee experience, and do it with responsibility to people, place, and community.

Our Vision

“To make Sri Lanka a place where no one has to settle for less than the world’s best coffee experience”

Our Mission

“As the largest cafe chain in Sri Lanka, we will ensure that we create and deliver the best value to all our stakeholders while being responsible for safeguarding the interests of the environment and the community”
