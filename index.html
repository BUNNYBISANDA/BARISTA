CH. 02 / WHAT MAKES US

Know us a little better.

For over 23 years, Barista has been at the heart of Sri Lanka's café culture — known for its branded customer service and promise of delivering a world-class café experience.

  1. 01

    Island-wide outlet coverage

    We've got you covered with many outlets scattered island-wide, guaranteeing that you will always find us nearby whenever you're craving a perfect cup of coffee.

  2. 02

    Highly skilled professional baristas

    We have highly skilled, VTA, and NVQ-certified professional baristas, ensuring that every visit to Barista is not just about the coffee but also about the unique connection and delight that comes with it.

  3. 03

    Barista branded customer service

    Our commitment is to deliver exceptional customer service and a coffee experience that stands out from the rest. We strive to create a memorable experience with every sip.

  4. 04

    Deploying new trends and technology

    We have implemented innovative technologies including the Mobile app, E-shop, Coffee Drive-thru, and store-within-stores to ensure a smooth and convenient experience for our customers.
