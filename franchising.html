CH. 05 / TWO PATHS

Two models. Choose your involvement.

MODEL A

FIFO

Franchise Invested, Franchise Operated.

You handle both the initial investment and the day-to-day operations — procurement, maintenance, staff, utilities, recruitment. Active daily involvement is required.

BEST FOR — Operators with F&B sector experience.

MODEL B

FICO

Franchise Invested, Company Operated.

You handle the initial investment. Barista runs the day-to-day — procurement, training, maintenance, staff, utilities, recruitment. Hands-off ownership.

BEST FOR — Busy professionals or business owners.

CH. 09 / SITE CRITERIA

What we look for in a location.

These criteria reflect what works for the brand and what protects your investment.

  1. 01

    Outlet between 1,400 and 1,800 square feet.

  2. 02

    Located on the main road, ideally on the right side.

  3. 03

    Ground floor, preferably with a horizontal layout.

  4. 04

    Width should exceed depth.

  5. 05

    6-7 parking spaces directly in front of the outlet.

  6. 06

    5-year rental agreement. Rent should not exceed LKR 350,000 in the specified areas.
